The Galleries

The Law Family Galleries are located in Lowry at the top of the escalator and are free to enter for all. Donations are welcome.

Galleries opening hours

Tues – Fri  | 11.00–17.00 
Sat- Sun | 10.00 – 17.00
Monday | closed 


We welcome everyone to The Law Family Galleries. You can find out more about which exhibitions are currently showing and what is upcoming, plus more information on how to plan your visit.

 

Visiting the Lowry Galleries this summer!!!

To manage capacities and make your visit enjoyable, we are allocating time slots for both LOWRY 360 and the Modern Life : The LS Lowry Exhibition and Quentin Blake and Me exhibitions. 

One FREE ticket will allow access to Modern Life: The LS Lowry Exhibition and Quentin Blake and Me, and you should plan about one hour for this part of your visit. There will be lots of free activities around the venue so you can of course make a day of it!  

LOWRY 360 is a separate FREE ticket with 15 minute time slots 

(Please remember to book separately for each of the timed sessions, as a ticket to one does not grant access to the other).

All our exhibitions are FREE, and we greatly appreciate any donations.

When you arrive at the Galleries Welcome Desk, our friendly staff will be there to guide you and answer any questions you might have.

We look forward to welcoming you soon!

(We will make every effort to keep to the  time slots but please bear with us at busy times).

 

Events in The Galleries 

Relaxed Session - This is a session for individuals and families with access requirements who would prefer to experience our Galleries in a calmer and less busy environment.
